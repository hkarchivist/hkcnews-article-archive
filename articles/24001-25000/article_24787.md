# 【眾新聞真相調查 】警察濫暴　社工爆頭


{{< video src="https://web.archive.org/web/20191114135209if_/https://www.youtube.com/embed/9TmWNlORbeI" >}}


自今年反逃犯條例修訂運動開始，不斷有指控指警方濫用暴力，最近輿論更指警員失控。有關這類指控，其實可以從多個事件中睇到真相。

譬如，2019年10月30日，在旺角雅蘭街休憩處，陣地社工Key無故被警察扑穿頭。我們仔細分解現場影片，發現警暴受害人不止Key，當時濫暴的警察最少達5人。我們用影片還原真相。

The Hong Kong Police Force (HKPF) has regularly been accused of committing police brutality ever since the beginning of the 2019 Anti-Extradition Bill Protests in Hong Kong, but recently the accusations have escalated to include the specific charge that the force has been acting "out of control."

Based on our analysis of a video that documented an encounter between the public and police, we can now corroborate at least in part the veracity of the allegations.

On 30 October 2019 a social worker - Key - was assaulted by the police during a public gathering at a park on Arran Street. However, further review of the video footage revealed that five more members of the public were also subjected to an abuse of powers by the HKPF.
