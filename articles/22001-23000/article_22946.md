# 7位前後任召集人同場　港人展示不屈服決心

<figure>
<img src="https://web.archive.org/web/20190818211434im_/https://www.hkcnews.com/news_新聞/hk-discuss/2019/08/818集會-逃犯條例-法政匯思-20190818200350_58df_large.jpg" alt="">
<figcaption>法政匯思七位前後任召集人：（左起）文浩正、梁允信、吳宗鑾、任建峰、Jason Y Ng、李安然及蔡騏。</figcaption>
</figure>

法政匯思現正在維園參與民間人權陣線的集會。我們由成立至今的7位現任和前任的召集人都在此，這是他們第一次一起出席同一個反修例活動。他們共同強調以下三點：

1. 2019年8月2日，香港民意研究所發表調查結果，7成9受訪對象支持成立獨立調查委員會，就整個反修例運動進行調查。這項合理訴求無疑獲壓倒性的民意支持，政府根本無任何理據漠視該訴求。

2. 港澳辦及解放軍已重複就運動及示威者公開發表評論。 反修例運動完全屬於香港的地方事務，港澳辦及解放軍的行徑，已涉嫌違反《基本法》第14及22條。

3. 面對解放軍的武力威脅，港人更應清晰表達我們的合理訴求，展示港人不會輕易屈服的決心。

The Progressive Lawyers Group has gathered at Victoria Park to take part in the Civil Human Rights Front assembly here. Our 7 Convenors, past and present, are here together. This is the first time they have appeared together in a Movement event. They wish to emphasise the following 3 points.

1. On 2 August 2019, the Hong Kong Public Opinion Research Institute issued a survey. 79% of the population support the setting up of an independent commission of inquiry into this whole saga. This is a sensible demand of an overwhelming majority of Hong Kong. There is simply no legitimate reason why it has not been acceded to.

2. The Hong Kong and Macau Affairs Office and the People’s Liberation Army have repeatedly commented on the saga and its participants. These are clearly internal or local matters and therefore such interference is prima facie in breach of Articles 14 and 22 of Basic Law of HK.

3. In view of the repeated threats of force by the People’s Liberation Army, the people of Hong Kong should state their reasonable demands louder and clearer than ever to show their unyielding determination.
