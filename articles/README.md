# 眾新聞文章備份 Citizen News HK Article Backup

Backup of all hkcnews.com articles

## Coverage:
- 49,118 articles backed up, from 2016-12-30 to 2022-01-03, out of 49,122 articles total on hkcnews.com
- 4 articles were deleted already on archive.org and could not be back up

## Format:
- Each article is in a separate file, in Markdown + YAML frontmatter format. 
- YAML frontmatter contains article metadata such as title, author, date, category, tags, url, original_url. 
- Article content is in Markdown format. 
- Only text content of article is backed up. Images, videos are not, but articles have `img src` attributes to the corresponding images hosted on archive.org.
