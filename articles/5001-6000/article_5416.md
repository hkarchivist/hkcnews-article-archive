# 悼念劉曉波：正義長存

[請支持《眾新聞》，成為訂戶](https://web.archive.org/web/20210702121319/https://www.hkcnews.com/aboutus/#subscribe)

【撰文：飄楊】   
   
 剛好今天早上祝賀好友生日快樂時說起心情沉重⋯⋯結果就是這一天了。   
   
 7月13日。2017年。   
 我們為這糜爛中共政權蹂躪、迫害的一顆善良、尊貴的靈魂悲痛、哀悼。雖然明知這一天早晚會到。   
 劉曉波留給中國的，是莫大的、他們不配的、迫切而深沉的愛。   
 但願我們紀念他的死。   
 更紀念他的生。   
   
 One of the most beautiful and venerable souls has just fallen.   
 The Chinese Nobel Peace Prize winner, Liu Xiaobo, died, after battling with final-stage liver cancer. Still under custody of the cruel communist China that convicted him of political subversion with harsh jail sentence for his urge for freedom and human rights, that blocked him from accepting the Nobel prize, that refused to release him for overseas treatment, against his wish and strong urge from dignitaries and people all over the world.......as a prisoner.   
 He gave his all, his freedom and his deepest love, to a regime that least deserves it.   
   
 RIP Mr Liu. Your love and deeds and spirit will remain among us.   
 The world shall remember your tragic death.   
 But we shall better remember your life.
